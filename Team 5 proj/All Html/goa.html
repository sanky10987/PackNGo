Holidays in GOA

Goa in Western India is a beach lover's paradise. Home to some of the most pristine and popular beaches in the country, Goa is distinctly divided in to North Goa and South Goa, with each part offering a unique Goan charm. Baga, Calangute, Anjuna and Candolim are some of the beaches that can be found in North Goa. Palolem, Varca, Colva and Majorda are some of the beaches that can be found in South Goa. While North Goa is characterized by bars, nightclubs and beach party, South Goa is more sober with quaint white sandy shores dotted with a few beach shacks. Make your next holiday to Goa.

About Goa India:

Goa is a real tropical holiday paradise with long sandy beaches ... located on the western coast of India in the coastal belt known as Konkan.

Panaji (Panjim) is the state capital located on the banks of the Mandovi river and Vasco, Margao, Mapusa and Ponda are the other major towns.

No other state in India offers such a beautiful variety of beaches. Here you will find more than 36 gorgeous beaches, each one with it's own flavor and charm.

You can visit this beautiful beach state at any month of the year. But the best time of year to visit is between the middle of September and the end of February, winter season, this is also the peak tourist season. Weather at this time is at its best to enjoy yourself with your friends and families on beautiful beaches. But, be ready to expect huge crowds on these beaches, and also book your hotels in advance before coming, even the rates in this peak season will be very high. But always remember, family and your friends are your number 1 priority, not the rates.

After all, more than anywhere else on planet earth, this is a place where people really know how to relax.
